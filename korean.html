Korean Noodle Soup



Step 1: Mix the sauce ingredient to make a paste

Step 2: Boil the noodles and veggies in the chicken broth

Step 3: Stir the sauce mixture into the broth until it dissolves

Ingredients:

  • Somen noodles (or Instant Noodles without the seasoning packs)
  • Pacific Foods Organic Low Sodium Chicken BrothÂ 
  • Kimchi, Gochujang, maple syrup, soy sauce, and garlic to make the sauce mixture
  • Baby bok choy (or any other greens you prefer)
