Spicy Fried Noodles

1. Boil the water and cook the noodles for about 5 mins depending on the kind of noodles you use. Meanwhile, cut the green pepper into tiny pieces, grate your carrot and put them into a small bowl or container. Crack an egg into a bowl and stir it.
2. Add the oil into a pan and heat it up. Add the pepper and carrot and some a teaspoon of salt into pan and stir fry them for about 5 minutes.
3. Add the noodles into the pain while adding soy sauce and the chili sauce to the amount you like. STIR Fry them. After 3-4mins, add the egg into the pan and stir fry some more.
