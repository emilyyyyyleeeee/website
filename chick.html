Butter Chicken

In a bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients with some seasoning. Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces and toss with the marinade. Cover and chill in the fridge for 1 hr or overnight. In a large, heavy saucepan, heat the oil. Add the onion, garlic, green chilli, ginger and some seasoning. Fry on a medium heat for 10 mins or until soft. Add the spices with the tomato purée, cook for a further 2 mins until fragrant, then add the stock and marinated chicken. Cook for 15 mins, then add any remaining marinade left in the bowl. Simmer for 5 mins, then sprinkle with the toasted almonds.

Ingredients:

  • 500g skinless boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 lemon , juiced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin; 2 tsp paprika; 1-2 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 200g natural yogurt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion , chopped; 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 green chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • thumb-sized piece ginger , grated
  • 1 tsp garam masala; 2 tsp ground fenugreek; 3 tbsp tomato purée
  • 300ml chicken stock; 50g flaked almonds, toasted
